About Dr. Allah Bakhsh

Your Pet's Health,
Our Passion

My name is Dr. Allah Bakhsh, a veterinary surgeon with over 13 years of hands-on experience treating and caring for companion animals and equines. My journey in veterinary medicine has taken me from the operating theatre to the field.

At Islamabad Pets Clinic on Murree Road, Bharakahu, my mission is to provide compassionate, high-quality veterinary care that every pet owner can rely on. I also actively educate pet owners through digital platforms so help is always within reach.
